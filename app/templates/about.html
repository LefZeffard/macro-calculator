About

This is a simple daily macro tracker made for people who are already comfortable tracking their own macros. I got tired of the paywalls in other apps like MyFitnessPal and wanted something much more straightforward: just log your meals and see your running totals for the day.

Add a meal, and your daily calories, protein, and fat will automatically update on the summary page. The goal is to keep tracking quick, simple, and easy.

Why no carbs?

Carbs can make things more complicated because different types of fiber can affect the true calorie value of total carbs. Instead of forcing a carb target, this app gives you the freedom to choose your protein and fat goals for a given calorie target. You can then fill the rest of your calories with carbs however you prefer.

Logging Options

By Servings

Use this when you already know how many servings you are going to eat. Enter the macros for one serving, then enter the number of servings, and the app will calculate the totals for you.

By Weight

Use this when you do not know the number of servings ahead of time, but you do know the serving size by weight. Enter the weight per serving and the amount you are eating, and the app will calculate the totals for you.

Quick Add

Use this when you already know the total calories, protein, and fat for a food or meal. This is the fastest option for logging something simple without extra calculations.
